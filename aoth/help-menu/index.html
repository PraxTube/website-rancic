HELP MENU

This help menu will provide you with the necessary knowledge about the game to get started. The following controls will move your plane.

  • Steer plane to left: A
  • Steer plane to right: D
  • Accelerate plane: W
  • Decelerate plane: S

There are two weapons in this game, bullets and rockets. Rockets insta kill their target on hit, while bullets can be fired in quick succession but deal little damage.

  • Fire bullet: SPACE
  • Fire rocket: LEFT MOUSE

Note that the rockets beneath your wings will reappear once your rockets are reloaded.

Finally, you also have a dodge mechanic which allows you to dodge any incoming projectiles for a few frames, this is also known as invincibility frames.

  • Dodge: RIGHT MOUSE
