H1: Minimalistic non-player characters are sufficient to foster immersion in games with a minimalist design style.

H2: Immersion is maintained when player expectations are met, and breaks when those expectations are violated.

RQ1: What types of NPC behavior contribute to the breakdown of player immersion?

RQ2: What NPC design patterns can be used to maintain or enhance player immersion?

RQ3: How can game designers influence or manage player expectations to support immersion?

RQ4: To what extent are player expectations shaped by prior experience versus intrinsic cognitive tendencies?
